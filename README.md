# 🚨 CIVIGUARD AI

### Multi-Agent Civic Risk Intelligence System

CIVIGUARD AI is a prompt-engineered decision support system designed to analyze crisis situations, simulate critical decisions, detect misinformation, and generate a unified Civic Threat Index.

The system transforms unstructured emergency descriptions into structured civic intelligence to help users understand risk levels and make safer decisions during emergencies.

---

# 📌 Problem Statement

During emergency situations, citizens often face three major problems:

• Panic and emotional decision-making  
• Lack of structured risk analysis  
• Rapid spread of misinformation

Existing AI tools typically generate conversational text but do not provide structured civic intelligence.

CIVIGUARD AI addresses this gap by combining structured prompt engineering with risk aggregation modeling.

---

# 🧠 System Overview

CIVIGUARD AI consists of three core intelligence engines:

### 1️⃣ Crisis Intelligence Engine
Analyzes emergency situations and extracts structured risk indicators such as hazard type, severity level, escalation probability, and urgency score.

### 2️⃣ Decision Simulation Engine
Simulates multiple decision options and evaluates their potential risks and consequences.

### 3️⃣ Misinformation Detection Engine
Analyzes messages and rumors to detect emotional manipulation, logical fallacies, and credibility issues.

---

# 📊 Civic Threat Index

The system aggregates multiple risk signals into a unified threat score.

Formula:

Threat Score =  
(Urgency × 10 + Escalation + (100 − Credibility)) ÷ 3

This produces a Civic Threat Index between **0 and 100**, representing the overall risk level of the situation.
